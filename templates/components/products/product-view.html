Explanation of ship out time:
  • Immediately: This item is in stock in your closest warehouse and will ship out same day (if the order is placed before 4pm EST)
  • 2-4 days: This item is in stock, but is not at your local warehouse. A warehouse transfer will be required before it can be shipped to you.
  • 1-4 days: This item is in stock, but has a pending order. A warehouse transfer may be required if the pending order goes through and the remaining stock is only located at your non-local warehouse.
  • 1 week: This item is currently in transit to our warehouse from the factory.
  • 1 month or more: This item is currently still in production at the factory. Any orders will be considered a pre-order, and the item will ship once production is completed and our warehouse receives it.
